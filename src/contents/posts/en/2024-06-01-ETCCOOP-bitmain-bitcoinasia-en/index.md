As Bitcoin Asia unfolded in the vibrant city of Hong Kong, the event served as a crucial convergence point for Bitcoin and blockchain enthusiasts, developers, and industry leaders. This year, the ETC Cooperative had a presence at the event, thanks to its partnership with Bitmain, the global leader in cryptocurrency mining hardware. This collaboration underscored their joint commitment to advancing the Ethereum Classic (ETC) ecosystem through the innovative ETC Grants DAO program.

![HK](/2-hk.jpeg "")

Bitcoin Asia brought together some of the brightest minds and most influential players in the cryptocurrency space. The event provided a platform for exploring cutting-edge layer 2 technologies, discussing regulatory trends, and fostering collaboration across the blockchain community. With Hong Kong as the host city, the event promised a dynamic environment for innovation and networking.

![HK](/3-hk.jpeg "")

The ETC Cooperative is dedicated to promoting the growth and sustainability of the Ethereum Classic blockchain. By participating in Bitcoin Asia Week, the ETC Cooperative aimed to showcase its initiatives, engage with key stakeholders, and drive discussions on the future of decentralized technologies.

Central to their mission was the ETC Grants DAO program, an initiative designed to fund and support projects that could enhance the Ethereum Classic network. 

Bitmain's role as a partner and host for the ETC Cooperative at Bitcoin Asia Week highlighted the importance of strategic alliances in the blockchain industry. Bitmain's expertise in mining technology and its extensive infrastructure provided invaluable support for the ETC Grants DAO program.

![HK](/4-hk.jpeg "")

The collaboration between the ETC Cooperative and Bitmain aims to leverage Bitmain's industry leadership to attract a diverse range of high-quality projects to the ETC Grants DAO program. This partnership not only strengthens the Ethereum Classic network but also promotes a robust and sustainable ecosystem.

The collaboration between the ETC Cooperative and Bitmain at Bitcoin Asia was just the beginning. Both organizations are committed to exploring further avenues for collaboration, with the goal of enhancing the Ethereum Classic ecosystem and promoting broader blockchain adoption.

![HK](/5-hk.jpeg "")

The ETC Grants DAO program aims to fund a diverse range of projects, from infrastructure improvements and security enhancements to innovative dApps and DeFi solutions. By fostering a supportive and collaborative environment, the ETC Cooperative and Bitmain aim to unlock the full potential of the Ethereum Classic blockchain.

The next event to network with the ETC Grants Dao https://etcgrantsdao.io/ , Bitmain https://www.bitmain.com/ , and the ETC Cooperative teams will be June 16-18 in Las Vegas, United States at the World Digital Mining Summit Global 2024 https://www.bitmain.com/wdmsGlobal/LasVegas  (WDMS Global 2024)
